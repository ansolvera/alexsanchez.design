FAQ

Common questions

To the right, you'll find answers to some commonly-asked client questions. If you have any other questions I haven't covered here, send me an email.

How much will my website cost?

Website designs are quoted based on the needs of each individual project. Each website is unique and requires different tools and considerations. The minimum price for any project is $1,900. This price may increase based upon your needs and the complexity of your final website.

I already have a domain name. Can I use it?

Absolutely. Your new website will have a temporary staging domain until it's time to finally go live.

Is the cost of hosting included?

No; the cost of hosting your website is not included in the price. After the design has been finalized, clients will be required to create a paid hosting account with Netlify, Kinsta, or Webflow. The cost of hosting ranges from $15 to $20 per month. In some cases, I may be able to work with existing hosting providers.

Can you maintain my site for me?

Yes, I offer ongoing website support in the form of a monthly retainer package. If you're interested, we can discuss this further upon completion of the project.
